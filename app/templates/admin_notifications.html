Airspace summary

A periodic global digest pushed to TRMNL/Vestaboard: the aircraft count over the window plus a “special news” line built from triggers you mark Include in the airspace summary. If none of those fired within summary_news_lookback_hours, the line falls back to “time since last <trigger>”. Default cadence 15 min (TRMNL's refresh).
